About CORi

Our Mission

Welcome to CORi! Our mission is to provide simple, free, and accessible online tools for everyone. We believe that converting and editing files shouldn't be complicated or expensive. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone looking to convert a photo, our tools are designed to be fast, secure, and easy to use.

What We Do

CORi offers a growing suite of file conversion and editing tools. From converting PDFs to images, compressing files to save space, or merging documents, we're building a one-stop solution for all your digital document needs.

Our platform is built on modern, secure technology. We prioritize your privacy, and all files uploaded to our servers are automatically deleted after a short period. Your data is never shared, sold, or looked at.

Our Story

CORi started as a small project to solve a simple problem: the lack of a reliable, free, and ad-light online file converter. Frustrated with existing solutions that were slow and cluttered, we decided to build our own. Today, CORi is growing to serve millions of users, but our core values remain the same: simplicity, security, and accessibility.
